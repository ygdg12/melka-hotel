Melka International Hotel — Luxury 4-Star Hotel in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Melka International Hotel is Addis Ababa's premier boutique 4-star hotel, located on Churchill Road in the Arada district. Established in 2022, the hotel offers 88 individually furnished rooms with fireplaces, city-view balconies, and laptop-friendly workspaces. Amenities include a rooftop restaurant and bar with panoramic views, a full spa with body treatments and facials, a sauna, wellness centre, free private parking, 24-hour front desk, room service, concierge, ATM, and currency exchange. Complimentary continental breakfast is served daily. Located 400 metres from Derg Monument and 7 km from Addis Ababa Bole International Airport.
